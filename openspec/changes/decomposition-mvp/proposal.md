# Prioritize structural decomposition MVP

## Why

The initial repository baseline documented a broad platform vision, but the
product strategy is now clearer. The first commercial priority is structural
decomposition and engineering refinement rather than documentation-first or
generalized all-purpose modeling breadth.

The chosen market posture is:

- professional desktop software for studios and specialist creators
- SaaS-assisted collaboration and orchestration for review and heavy compute

The chosen technical priorities are:

- ZBrush-led interoperability first
- production-grade sculpt-assisted authoring
- deep CAE integration
- first-release outputs focused on part structure, runner data, and CNC/tooling
  handoff

## What changes

- Update requirements and architecture documents to reflect the new product
  priorities
- Introduce an MVP scope document centered on part splitting and structure
  engineering
- Introduce a first-pass domain model draft for the decomposition-first product
  slice
- Update capability specs for design workbench, manufacturing engineering, and
  assembly knowledge to reflect the chosen release priorities

## Expected impact

- More focused implementation planning for the first executable product slice
- Better alignment between desktop UX, geometry-domain design, and manufacturing
  outputs
- Reduced risk of scope sprawl during early implementation
