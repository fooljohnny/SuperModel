## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Hybrid commercial delivery

The platform SHALL support a desktop-first professional product paired with an
optional SaaS collaboration layer for review, asset synchronization, and remote
compute workloads.

#### Scenario: Work offline, then sync

- **WHEN** a studio designer works locally on structural decomposition and
  connector refinement
- **THEN** the desktop application remains fully usable without continuous
  connectivity
- **AND** approved revisions, review artifacts, and compute jobs can later sync
  to the collaboration service when the project policy allows.
